What We Offer

Our Services

Specialized residential care programs designed for Houston's highest-need youth — delivered in luxurious, private homes with clinical precision and genuine compassion.

Service 01

High-Acuity Residential Treatment Care

Cool Living Quarters is a licensed Residential Treatment Center — not a standard group home. We serve youth with complex behavioral, emotional, and psychiatric needs who require a higher level of structured residential support.

Our clinical team delivers evidence-based therapeutic programming within a home environment that prioritizes dignity, comfort, and individualized care.

  • Licensed RTC-level behavioral health programming
  • Individual, group, and family therapy integration
  • Trauma-informed care frameworks
  • Coordination with placing agencies and guardians
  • Educational support and life skills development

Service 02

Authorized Crisis Intervention

Unlike standard group homes, Cool Living Quarters is authorized to deliver clinical crisis interventions on-site. Our staff are trained and certified in approved crisis response protocols, ensuring residents receive immediate, safe, and therapeutic de-escalation — without the disruption of unnecessary emergency transports.

  • On-site authorized crisis intervention protocols
  • Certified staff in de-escalation and safety planning
  • 24/7 on-call clinical coverage
  • Coordination with mental health emergency services
  • Detailed incident documentation and agency reporting

Service 03

Human Trafficking Recovery Services

We provide a specialized recovery track for youth survivors of human trafficking — one of the most under-served and vulnerable populations in the child welfare system. Our program delivers trauma-specific therapy, safety planning, and survivor-centered support within a highly private, secure residential setting.

  • Trauma-specific programming for trafficking survivors
  • Safety planning and risk assessment protocols
  • Coordination with law enforcement and victim advocacy partners
  • High-privacy residential placement in upscale neighborhoods
  • Survivor-centered goal planning and aftercare support

Service 04

Luxury Home Environment

Our homes are intentionally located in Houston's upscale residential neighborhoods — providing the privacy, space, and aesthetic comfort that supports genuine healing. Every home is professionally appointed with spacious common areas, private bedrooms, and all the comforts a youth deserves.

  • Spacious homes in upscale Houston neighborhoods
  • Private bedrooms and comfortable communal spaces
  • Full home amenities — kitchens, backyards, living areas
  • Discreet, secure locations for maximum resident privacy
  • Home visits and tours available for placing agencies
